OR=1 Exposure does not affect odds of outcome

OR>1 Exposure associated with higher odds of outcome

OR<1 Exposure associated with lower odds of outcome

The Confusion Matrix created has four different quadrants:

False Negative (Top-Left Quadrant) - Predicted to be on the bench, but started

False Positive (Top-Right Quadrant) - predicted to start, but on the bench

True Negative (Bottom-Left Quadrant) - predicted to be on the bench, was on the bench

True Positive (Bottom-Right Quadrant) - predicted to start, did start the game

We want to maximise precision and recall, meaning that the goal is for the False Postive and Negative to be as small as possible.

The model has recall > precision for the goal of the analysis. This tells us that the model is better at predicting whether or not a player will start over whether or not a player is benched.
