About me

My name is Tom Edwards and I am aspiring to be a data scientist. I completed a Bachelor's degree in Science where I majored in Statistics as well as a Graduate certificate in data science. I have developed the right skills and knowledge to be a great asset to any data team. The one thing I love the most about data science and analystics is the multiple ways you can uncover the root causes of problems with data. I have recently been teaching myself about machine learning and have multiple skill in R, Python, SQL, Excel, Tableau and PowerBI which I hope are shown well through the various projects I have uploaded.

One other aspect of data science that I really like is the creative and descriptive side of it. Creating reports and dashboards is a great way to express yourself, the company and most importantly the data and is a great creative outlet for a numbers based job.

A lot of these projects that have been initially uploaded are sports centric. Other than data science, sport is one of my great passions. I have done research on a couple of things in Fantasy Premier League as well as some analysis of AFL teams at different grounds. My two teams in the Premier League and the AFL are Newcastle United and Collingwood Magpies, respectively.
